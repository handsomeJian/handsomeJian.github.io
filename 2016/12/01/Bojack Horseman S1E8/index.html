Bojack Horseman S1E8

This episode tells the stories happened between Bojack and his friends when he was younger. We finally know how Bojack became a famous star.

I always think that the main reason you learn a language is to express yourself better. I feel this point especially when I have something in my heart but don’t know how to describe the feelings or when I want to show my opinion but just cannot find a proper way to say it clearly. That’s really uncomfortable when you know what you are just like a child in a kindergarden as you speak.

I really want to say something about this episode because it’s so heavy. Can you imagine someone never forgives you even when he’s gonna die and someone wants you to regret in the rest of your life? It’s horrible.

Though I thought not forgiving is not proper but BoJack deserves it. Just as Herb said, what he hates is not that Bojack didn’t left his job but is that Bojack didn’t even call him for 20 years after the event. He need a friend but BoJack left at the time Herb needs him most.

Also I think the last sentence under is a great comment on Bojack Horseman.

    Wouldn’t that make a bicycle?

That’s you. That’s BoJack Horseman.
