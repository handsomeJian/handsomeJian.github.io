Bojack Horseman S1E12

This is the last episode of the series. I won’t see next season’s Bojack Horseman because I really don’t like this kind of stories.

However, in this episode, Bojack did change a lot and it makes me feel he’s good again……And I can’t believe that Bojack got cast in Secretariat. It’s his ever-wanted dream. Also he can work with Diane again.

At the end of the episode, Bojack and Diane sat on the roof, watching the sky full of stars. Bojack said to Diane:

I really wanted you to like me, Diane.
I know.

Again I feel that it’s hard to describe your feelings, no matter in which kind of languages. Maybe the basic aim of learning a language is just to show your feelings to others well. You ask me what I am feeling now? I would say:

I feel myself.
