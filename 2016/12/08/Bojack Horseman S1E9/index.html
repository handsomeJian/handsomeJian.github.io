Bojack Horseman S1E9

What would you do if your favorite woman is going to marry another man? Bojack want to ruin the wedding. He just don’t admit that he loves Diane and all the things he wanted to do are out of jealous.

People all get nervous or afraid when they facing the important time of their lives. Diane feel nervous about the coming wedding(even it’s one year later), and her hesitation makes Mr. Peanutbutter nervous too. But the “accident” Bojack made just make their progress going faster and they put forward the wedding to next Saturday.

Bojack said Diane deserves the best. I don’t know whether Mr. Peanutbutter is the best but I truly know that Bojack is not.
