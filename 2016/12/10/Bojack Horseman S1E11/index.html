Bojack Horseman S1E11

I think I shouldn’t watch this episode because it’s so dark and make me feel so sad. And do not take drugs…

After taking drugs, Bojack saw the life he wanted. It was peaceful and sweet, living in forest with his wife and daughter. Actually that’s also the life I want, always want. But for Bojack, it’s too late. He cannot live such life anymore.

At the end of the episode, Bojack keep asking or maybe more specifically, begging for Diane to say that it’s not too late and he is still a good person. I feel deeply moved when I see this scene. A person who just want to be good but cannot. And finally Diane didn’t say a word.

Sentences:

  • People respond to the flawed(not perfect) portrait I painted of you. They see themselves in it.

  • When people find out that someone like you, who seems larger than life, is actually just as wounded and vulnerable(weak or delicate) as they are, it makes them feel less lonely.

  • Look, all publicity is good publicity.

  • Now, I spend a lot of time with the real me, and believe me, nobody’s gonna love that guy.

  • These are what I give college students so when they come to, their papers have written themselves.

  • As you know, I was hurt, but then I realized that’s just how you are. You know, and maybe I just need to stop expecting you to be a good person, so that way, I won’t get hurt when you’re not.

  • This is all I am and all I’ll ever be.

  • All you can do is be good to the people in your life, and keep your heart open.

  • What are you thinking about? Oh, just how nice things could’ve been if you had chosen this life.

  • I just hated reading that book because I hated feeling like that’s how you saw me. Because I guess you know me better than anybody, and if you think that…

  • I know that I can be selfish and narcissistic(feeling good about yourself) and self-destructive, but underneath all that, deep down, I’m a good person.
